How to get here?

For the fast moving people of our generation I have devised a tabular format,so that people can go through it easily:
Mode of conveyance Description
By road After landing at Netaji International Airport you need to take a bus/car and take a 46km journey to our town
By rail The distance between Howrah to Hooghly District Head Quarter Chinsurah approx 35.6 K.M. by train. From Howrah Station avail the Local train to reach Chinsurah(Chuchura). Time taken to reach 1 hour approximate.
