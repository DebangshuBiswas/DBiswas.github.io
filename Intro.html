PC vs Consoles(my all time favourite topic)

Since I am an avid gamer and also I have done a lot of research on the topic,so I do think I have the right to discuss the topic with you guys.
PC
  • One can perform other tasks excluding gaming
  • Servicing of PCs are easier in comparison to consoles
  • As the number of fans or cooling equipment need not be reduced it can be used for longer periods of time
  • Control the way you play: gaming peripherals
  • PC games cost less
  • The visuals are better
  • You can customize PC hardware
Console
  • You don't have to upgrade hardware
  • Brings multiplayer to masses
  • Console-exclusive titles
  • Wireless controllers
